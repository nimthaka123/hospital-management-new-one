Terms and conditions

  • appoinment:A scheduled time when a patient meets with a doctor for consultation, examination, or treatment.
  • booking:The process of reserving a time slot for an appointment with a doctor.
  • calender:A schedule or timeline that shows available and booked times for doctors, used to manage and view appointments.
  • patient:The individual seeking medical consultation or treatment.
  • doctor:A medical professional providing consultation, examination, or treatment.
  • time slot:A specific period during which a doctor is available for appointments.
  • confermation:An acknowledgment that an appointment has been successfully booked and scheduled.
  • cancelation:The process of removing or rescheduling a previously made appointment.
